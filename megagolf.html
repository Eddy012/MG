How to Play

Goal: Get the ball to reach the hole in as few holes as possible.
To hit the ball, click on the ball and hold down, drag and release.
Power can be adjusted by how far you drag back.


Ball Types

Standard:

The standard golf ball

Grenade:

Destroys boxes if you press space bar as the ball is moving

Gravity Warp:

Anti gravity ball moves in the opposite way of Gravity

Sticky:

Sticks to the first thing it touches

Bouncy:

Has increased bounce



Special Obstacles

Sand Dune:

Ball slows down if it hits the sand dune

Pond:

Ball returns to previous spot and the stoke count increases by one

Portal:

Ball travels through the orange portal and out of the blue
